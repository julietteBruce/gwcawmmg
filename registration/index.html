Registration

There will be a limited amount of funding for junior participants. We expect to be able to provide partial travel support for some participants from within the United States. Accommodation for funded participants will be arranged by the organizers for the nights of Friday, April 12 and Saturday, April 13.

Researchers from generally underrepresented groups are strongly encouraged to apply, and will be given priority when it comes to funding decisions.

We wish to make this conference as inclusive and welcoming as possible. If there is anything the organizers can do in this direction to make attending the conference easier or more pleasent please contact us. In particular, if you are in need of child care during the conference, please contact one of the organizers and we will help with arrangements.

If you wish to participate in the workshop please complete the GoogleForm below by February 28, 2019. (If you have trouble with the embedded form below, you can also register by completing the form here.)
