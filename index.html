Graduate Workshop in Commutative Algebra for Women & Mathematicians of Other Minority Genders

We will hold a workshop at the University of Minnesota, April 12-14, 2019, focusing on forming a community of women and non-binary researchers interested in commutative algebra by learning about specific topics in commutative algebra from a diverse group of prominent active researchers.

This workshop is organized by Christine Berkesch, Juliette Bruce, and Patricia Klein in cooperation with the AWM.
